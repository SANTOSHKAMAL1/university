Implementation of SAMUDI Courses as University Open Electives

In accordance with the National Education Policy (NEP)-2020, Higher Education Institutions are required to provide flexible, multidisciplinary, and holistic learning opportunities to students through Open Electives. The University has conceptualized SAMUDI (Skills, Attitude, Mindset, Understanding, Digital and Interdisciplinary) courses as University Open Electives.

Understanding the SWAYAM Platform

SWAYAM (Study Webs of ActiveLearning for Young Aspiring Minds) is the national online learning platform of the Government of India, initiated by the Ministry of Education. It offers high-quality, credit-bearing online courses developed by premier institutions and universities across the country.

Eligibility

  • All UG and PG students eligible
  • Use official university email ID
  • Local Chapter ID: 7730
  • Register for SAMUDI categories

Registration Process

  • Visit www.swayam.gov.in
  • Sign in with university email
  • Select JAIN University - Chapter 7730
  • Attend orientation session

Evaluation Pattern

  • 70% End-term examination
  • 30% Internal/Assignment evaluation
  • Min 40% in internal assessments
  • Min 40% overall for credit transfer

Credit Transfer

  • Recorded in Academic Bank of Credits
  • Reflected in semester mark sheet
  • Lifelong learning recognition
  • NEP 2020 aligned

2. Course Identification & Approval Process

  • 1 Departments identify relevant credit-bearing SWAYAM courses aligned with SAMUDI categories
  • 2 Proposed courses scrutinized and recommended by Department Board of Studies (BoS)
  • 3 Forwarded to Office of Academics for compliance review and Academic Council approval

Academic & Examination Guidelines

End Semester Examination

The final examination will be conducted by the University. Details pertaining to the examination schedule, mode, and evaluation process will be communicated.

Introduction of CETT

Centre for Education Transformation Through Technology (CETT) at Jain (Deemed-to-be University) is dedicated to redefining education through the use of advanced technology. Located within the university's innovative ecosystem, CETT aims to enhance quality of education by integrating digital tools and modern teaching methods. It strives to create an inclusive, engaging, and effective learning environment.

CETT collaborates with academicians, technologists, and industry experts to conduct research, develop digital curricula, and implement projects demonstrating technology's transformative impact on education. A significant focus of CETT is faculty upskilling, ensuring educators are well-prepared to guide learners in the digital age. Additionally, CETT emphasizes promoting cross-disciplinary skills among learners, preparing them to succeed in various interconnected fields. CETT at Jain (Deemed-to-be University) is committed to equipping learners with essential digital-era skills, fostering a culture of continuous learning and innovation.
